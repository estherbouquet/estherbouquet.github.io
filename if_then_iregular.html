Internship report and reflexion about Iregular, a creative studio which works at the intersection of art and technology. The studio approaches design using a code-driven and real-time mindset. Their work mixes light, sound and geometry with mathematics and algorithms. They create what they called "systems" with infinite possibilities, and prefer creating by making. By questionning the use of code in design through the writing of this internship report, I have decided to also question its use through my own practice. The pictures presented in this editorial design were randomly placed by a Processing program developped for this occasion.
