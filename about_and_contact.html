ABOUT ME:

After graduating a two-year vocational degree in space design at Olivier de Serres and I am currently studying graphic design in a multidisciplinary master degree, at LAAB, in Rennes.

I am really interested in art history and design theory. I also like mixing borders of graphic and space design through interdisciplinary projects, interactive installations, or wayfinding. I enjoy exhibition scenography, the event quality of spaces but also digital and interactive medias.

Always on the lookout for graphic and space design news, I took part in several workshops where I fisrt discovered interactive light experimentation and then various techniques, various pratices such as typography or programming languages.
