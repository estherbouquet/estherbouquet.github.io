ODIL

projects

Thanks to its mediation and interpreter skills, ODIL (The Essor Affiches newspaper’s Dynamic Operator of Local Information) offers visitors an experience to discover (again) the printed press in order to highlight its importance. The development of online press has indeed created many deep changes in the way we receive information and has birthed a certain distance between the reader and the content. Embracing the challenges linked to this revolution, ODIL tends to capture the sensitive nature of the written press focusing on the manipulation of paper.

Art direction, conception and scenography developed with Léa Belzunces and Déborah-Loïs Séry.

Exhibited at the Biennale Internationale Design 2019 (Saint-Etienne, France).
