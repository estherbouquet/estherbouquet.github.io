transrisographiques

projects

Every day for one week, we designed one poster for one group of music invited to the 37th edition of Les Transmusicales in Rennes, a music festival. This one-week workshop with SuperTerrain led us to create and design lots of posters in a very short time in order to explore and discover the technical and plastical capabilities of the risograph printing.

It was then developed in an exhibition, divided in different poles to highlight the variety and wealth of the printed posters. The exhibition displayed the several steps of the risograph printing with first the masters used for the different color layers, then the macules used to chock the printing and finally the finished posters.

Exhibited at Galerie LENDROIT (Rennes, France) in February 2017.
