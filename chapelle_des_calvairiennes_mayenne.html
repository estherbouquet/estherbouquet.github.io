art in context

projects

This book looks back at the analyse and critique work produced around the installations exhibited in la Chapelle des Calvairiennes (Mayenne, France). The concept is based on spatial sensations: when we visit an exhibition, we explore, wander, turn around the artworks; we synthetise them to better appreciate them in their globality but also to appreciate them in their details to understand them intimately, closely.

art in context is thus a guide, that tries to reflect both the past spatial feelings of the exhibitions and the mental wandering of the texts created, generated by those same exhibitions.
