INTERNSHIP AT IREGULAR — Delete

DELETE is an immersive, site-specific, interactive experience for audiences from 8 to 12 years old. In 6 parts (2 websites and 4 rooms) DELETE asks: In a world where the line between what’s real and what’s virtual has disappeared, what part of what makes us human do we want to keep? During my internship I essentially worked on the scenography of the different rooms and I took part in the experimental process, testing the light effects and interaction.
