delete

projects

Immersive, site-specific, interactive experience for audiences from 8 to 12 years old. In 6 parts (2 websites and 4 rooms) DELETE asks: in a world where the line between what’s real and what’s virtual has disappeared, what part of what makes us human do we want to keep? During my internship I essentially worked on the scenography of the different rooms and I took part in the experimental process, testing the light effects and interaction.

delete was presented at Eastern Bloc (Montréal, Canada) in November 2016. This project received the Infopress Boomerang 2017 award (Non-Commercial Interactive Environment Grand Prix), the Numix 2017 Grand Prix and Best Interactive Experiential Production.
