ne nous froissons pas

projects

Interactive installation enabling a new experience of touch. Each visitor is invited to manipulate a sheet of paper (scrunching it, scratching, folding, rolling, stroking it, ...) creating a unique lighting reaction. Each one of those interactions are then graphically translated and saved, generating a collection of the individual experiences.
