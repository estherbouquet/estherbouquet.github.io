INTERNSHIP AT IREGULAR — AIM Festival

I took part in this project at the beginning of my internship at Iregular. It was already designed and built, I just helped during the set up for the AIM Festival. FORWARD is an installation of tunnels that generate infinite ways to get to a distant point on the horizon. This project is about the future becoming the present, and disappearing.
