capes de survie

projects

capes de survie is a dancing performance. The show is about the encounter of capes, lights and bodies, with homemade lighting devices that were conceived for the occasion.

As the dancers, we were manipulating the light whether it was in our hands, on our head, or on objects. The light then became a volume, a material, a mouvement to create a dance show augmented by the light which was not just here as a technical element anymore.

Performed during Ici&Demain 2015 (Paris, France).
